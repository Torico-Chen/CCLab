Place the flour mound on a large cutting board. Then use your fingers or a spoon to make a good-sized well in the middle of the flour mound (kind of like a volcano). Add the egg to the center of the well. Sprinkle the egg with salt and drizzle with olive oil. Using a fork, start whisking the egg. Then, once it's combined, begin gradually whisking some of the flour around into the egg mixture, adding more and more until the egg mixture is nice and thick. Using your hands, fold the remaining dough together until combined. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes or until the dough is smooth and elastic, roll the dough into a ball, wrap tightly in plastic wrap, and let the dough sit at room temperature.
