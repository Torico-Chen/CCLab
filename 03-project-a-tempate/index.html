Scientific Name:

[ Stellar Ember ]

Discovered by / at:

Voyager from M34-4602151-B discovered in 2025

*Believe it or not*

People who encounter Stellar Ember will gain enlightenment and inspiration, and their minds will be closely connected with the power of the universe, bringing creativity and inner peace.

Habitat:

Stellar Ember likes to stay away from noisy and crowded places, and they often find habitats in quiet corners of the universe. They travel through nebulae or hide on the surface of stars, trying to scare passing travelers.

Appearance:

Stellar Ember's appearance is like a sticky star, breathing life into it like fire. Its body is composed of small, bright particles. At its core, nuclear fusion reactions occur continuously, compressing hydrogen atoms into helium and releasing huge amounts of energy.

Key Characteristics:

  • Only be captured by the black hole.
  • Two mode.
  • Can generate stars.
  • The core is the most vulnerable point.

What do we know so far about the creature?

Stellar Ember was born in one of the oldest regions of the universe, in a burst of radiant energy as pressure and temperature rose within the primordial nebula and the first flashes of nuclear fusion were ignited. Stellar Ember is a lonely wanderer. It drifts among the stars, observing the revolutions and dance of the planets. Billions of years later, it arrives at a vibrant stellar nursery where new stars are just beginning to emerge. Fascinated by swirling colors and high-energy charged particles, Stellar Ember senses untapped creative potential. It began to dance in the clouds, its shimmering body compressing slightly, igniting the gas. Soon, the first STAR of the new generation was formed.
