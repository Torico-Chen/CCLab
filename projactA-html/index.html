Habitat:

Stellar Ember likes to stay away from noisy and crowded places, and they often find habitats in quiet corners of the universe. They travel through nebulae or hide on the surface of stars, trying to scare passing travelers.

Appearance:

Stellar Ember's appearance is like a sticky star, breathing life into it like fire. Its body is composed of small, bright particles. At its core, nuclear fusion reactions occur continuously, compressing hydrogen atoms into helium and releasing huge amounts of energy. Main feature:

Some predatory creatures will attack it, trying to harvest its huge energy, and there are also alien creatures trying to capture the ashes of the star, but Stellar Ember will only be captured by the black hole. When it approaches a black hole, it triggers a stress state and is torn into a series of fragments by the extremely strong gravitational force, forming an accretion disk. In the accretion disk, the stellar ember continues to accelerate, rotate, and heat, and eventually passes through the black hole's event horizon and is permanently trapped inside the black hole.

What do we know about this creature so far?

Stellar Ember was born in one of the oldest regions of the universe, in a burst of radiant energy as pressure and temperature rose within the primordial nebula and the first flashes of nuclear fusion were ignited. Stellar Ember is a lonely wanderer. It drifts among the stars, observing the revolutions and dance of the planets. Billions of years later, it arrives at a vibrant stellar nursery where new stars are just beginning to emerge. Fascinated by swirling colors and high-energy charged particles, Stellar Ember senses untapped creative potential. It began to dance in the clouds, its shimmering body compressing slightly, igniting the gas. Soon, the first STAR of the new generation was formed. Since then, Stellar Ember has made it its life's work to inspire new life and creativity wherever it goes. It will look for developing star systems, galaxies in the midst of artistic upheavals, and even fledgling civilizations just discovering their cultural voice. Stellar Ember will nourish these emerging creative works with its graceful dance and soothing pulses of light, just as it nurtured the stars long ago.
